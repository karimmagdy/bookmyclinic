## What this app is
A two-stage build of BookMyClinic: Stage 1 delivers the patient-facing booking flow — slot listing (Sun–Thu, 4pm–9pm, 60-min slots), name+phone booking form, and confirmation page. Stage 2 adds an admin dashboard with password-protected login and a sorted list of all bookings. The app uses Next.js 15 App Router, Tailwind CSS, Supabase for storage, and deploys to Vercel. No online payment — patients pay 100 EGP cash at the clinic.
